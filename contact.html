Hub news

Stay up to date on new scenario rounds, upcoming meetings, and results. All are welcome to join the European Scenario Hub mailing list. Please include your name and a brief sentence about your interest in the Hub (so that we can exclude bots).

New enquiries

We welcome all enquiries about the European Scenario Hub. We are particularly keen to hear from modellers interested in participating.

To get in touch, please:

  • Send us an email at mail@covid19scenariohub.eu

  • Open a Github Issue

Existing Hub participants

Please join our Slack workspace, where we encourage Hub participants and users to discuss modelling work. Please get in touch for the invite link.

For technical issues using the Scenario Hub (including this website) please open an Issue in our Github repository.

About us

Both the European Scenario and Forecast Hubs are run in collaboration between the Epiforecasts team, part of the Centre for Mathematical Modelling of Infectious Diseases at the London School of Hygiene & Tropical Medicine, and the European Centre for Disease Control and Prevention (ECDC).

The LSHTM team working on Hub research and development includes Katharine Sherratt (@kathsherratt), Hugo Gruson (@grusonh), and Sebastian Funk (@sbfnk).
